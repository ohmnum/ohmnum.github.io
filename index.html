About

Research shaped by real engineering constraints.

My work sits between mechanical engineering, artificial intelligence, and human-centered technical workflows. I am especially interested in AI that does not only predict, but also explains, structures, and collaborates with engineers.

This site is being rebuilt as a concise home for my research themes, selected projects, publications, and professional links.
