About

Research records from graduate and postdoctoral work.

This site records research conducted during my graduate and postdoctoral studies. I received my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Seoul National University in 2019 and continued my Ph.D. and postdoctoral research at HAI LAB in Seoul, South Korea.

My research has focused on integrating domain knowledge into artificial intelligence. Building on this foundation, I now work on refining domain knowledge into structured knowledge graphs and integrating them with LLM and RAG techniques to develop intelligent AI agents.

Research

Research areas

PHM

Prognostics and Health Management

PHM monitors, evaluates, and predicts the health of mechanical, electronic, and industrial systems. My PHM work has focused on rotating machines and thermal power plants, with attention to anomaly detection, diagnosis, analysis, prognosis, and maintenance decision support.

01

PHM

Fault diagnosis and predictive maintenance for rotating machines and thermal power plants, including boiler tube leakage detection and multichannel anomaly detection.

02

Domain Knowledge Informed Neural Networks

Combining deep learning with engineering knowledge to improve generalization, physical consistency, and interpretability.

03

AI-driven Design

Applying AI to design generation, evaluation, and optimization, including inverse modeling, reinforcement learning, and design parameter optimization.

04

LLM Applications in Engineering

Developing engineering datasets, multimodal workflows, and domain-specialized LLM/RAG systems for design and signal analysis.
